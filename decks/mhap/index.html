The cost of friction

Right now, friction is singlehandedly responsible for the loss of more than 40% of business.

When buying is slow or clunky, fans may not complain about it, they may just abandon the idea of buying. The worst part is, the money does not go elsewhere, it simply remains in their pockets. This is lost revenue the arena will never see.

The cure, and the lever we control

Removing friction smoothens out every business stream.

The pattern holds across everything the platform will sell. By improving the buying experience, removing friction, making it faster and easier for the fans, they will buy more. This is the part of the revenue we have in our hands.

+42%

Food and beverages

The NBA data shows us there is 42% more F&B sales when the queue is halved. This is the biggest single win, and the one mobile ordering and cashless payments go straight at.

+29%

Merchandise

Scope

Both products, built as one.

One system and one journey only work if both products are designed together. So we build them side by side, run everything through LOVE, and keep the experience whole.

This phase starts with an online kick-off and ends with the in-person sessions. Naturally, with the help of AI, we can create near-fully-functional prototypes, in code, just in time to iterate when we meet face-to-face.

During the workshop, with further help of AI, as we discuss and iterate the wireframes live with the clickable prototype, another team member is live-vibe-coding and refining it, allowing us to see the reflection of our discussion in near-real-time.

By the end of the workshop, we have a fully defined and discussed set of wireframes that will serve as the source-of-truth for the scope of work of the project ahead.
